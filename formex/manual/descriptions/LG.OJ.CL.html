LG.OJ.CL

[element]

Language of a consolidated act

The LG.OJ.CL element is used to mark up the language(s) of a consolidated act.

Model

<xd:element name="LG.OJ.CL" type="xd:string" />

Used by

LG.OJ.CL OJ.CL

General rules

Element

The allowed values, based on ISO 639-1, are listed in the t_language simple type. Various values are separated by ';'.
