NO.ISBN

[element]

ISBN (International Standard Book Number)

The NO.ISBN element is used to mark up the ISBN number of a general publication.

Model

<xd:element name="NO.ISBN" >
  <xd:simpleType >
    <xd:restriction base="xd:string" >
      <xd:pattern value="((\d|\-){12}(\d|X))|((\d|\-){16}(\d|X))" />
    </xd:restriction>
  </xd:simpleType>
</xd:element>

Used by

BIB.GEN.PUB OTH.PUB.CL

General rules

Element

ISBN is an international code which allows to uniquely identify an individual publication. It consists of eleven digits and a check sum which may be a digit or the letter 'X'.

Since 1 January 2007 ISBNs have consisted of 13 digits, the last one being a check digit.
