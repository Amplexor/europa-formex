TI.GR.CN.UNIT

[element]

Title of a group of units in the combined nomenclature

The TI.GR.CN.UNIT element is used to mark up the title of a group of units in the combined nomenclature.

Model

<xd:element name="TI.GR.CN.UNIT" type="t_btx" />

Used by

GR.CN.UNIT TI.GR.CN.UNIT

General rules

Element

The TI.GR.CN.UNIT element may contain text or any of the elements defined in the t_btx complex type.
