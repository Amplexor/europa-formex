YEAR

[element]

Publication year of a document or an OJ

The YEAR element is used to mark up the publication year of either a document or an Official Journal.

Model

<xd:element name="YEAR" >
  <xd:simpleType >
    <xd:restriction base="xd:string" >
      <xd:pattern value="\d{4}" />
    </xd:restriction>
  </xd:simpleType>
</xd:element>

Used by

BIB.CASE BIB.ECR BIB.SE DOC.CORR.SE DOCUMENT.REF DOCUMENT.REF.CONS NO.DOC NO.DOC PUBLICATION.REF.SE REF.ECR REF.OJ SPEC.ED

General rules

Element

This element always consists of a four-digit numerical value.
