OP.CMP

[element]

Operator for comparisons

Within a mathematical formula the element OP.CMP stands for a symbol of comparison.

General rules

Element

The mandatory attribute TYPE specifies the type of comparison. It may contain one of the following values:

  • EQ: equal to: =,
  • LT: less than: <,
  • GT: greater than: >,
  • NE: not equal to: ≠,
  • LE: less than or equal to: ≤,
  • GE: greater than or equal to: ≥,
  • AP: approximately: ≈,
  • EQV: not equal to: ≡.
