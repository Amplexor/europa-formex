GR.MOD.ACT

[element]

Group of modifying acts

The GR.MOD.ACT element is used to mark up the modifying acts that refer to the same document. This document may be:

  • a basic act, or
  • a modifying act.

Model

<xd:element name="GR.MOD.ACT" >
  <xd:complexType >
    <xd:sequence >
      <xd:element ref="MOD.ACT" maxOccurs="unbounded" />
    </xd:sequence>
  </xd:complexType>
</xd:element>

Used by

FAM.COMP MOD.ACT

General rules

Element

The GR.MOD.ACT element contains at least one MOD.ACT element.

Example

<GR.MOD.ACT>
