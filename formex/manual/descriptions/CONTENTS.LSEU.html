CONTENTS.LSEU

[element]

Content of a legislation summary

The element contains the provisions of a legislative summary.

Model

<xd:element name="CONTENTS.LSEU" >
  <xd:complexType >
    <xd:sequence >
      <xd:element ref="HEADER.LSEU" minOccurs="0" maxOccurs="unbounded" />
      <xd:choice minOccurs="0" maxOccurs="unbounded" >
        <xd:element ref="ADDITIONAL.DOCS.LSEU" />
        <xd:element ref="REFERENCE.TABLE" />
        <xd:element ref="REFERENCES.LSEU" />
        <xd:element ref="REFERRED.DOCS.LSEU" />
        <xd:element ref="SUMMARY.LSEU" />
        <xd:element ref="MENU.LSEU" minOccurs="0" />
      </xd:choice>
    </xd:sequence>
  </xd:complexType>
</xd:element>

Used by

LSEU

General rules

Element

It is composed of the following elements:

  • a mandatory HEADER.LSEU element;

  • a sequence of REFERENCES.LSEU), which is optional, optionally the contents of the summary itself (SUMMARY.LSEU) and optionally an unlimited selection of ADDITIONAL.DOCS.LSEU, REFERENCE.TABLE or REFERRED.DOCS.LSEU elements, or the element MENU.LSEU).
