LG.PUB

[element]

Language of a general publication

The LG.PUB element is used to mark up the language(s) of a general publication.

Model

<xd:element name="LG.PUB" type="xd:string" />

Used by

BIB.ECR BIB.SE HEADER.SUMMARY.SE LG.PUB OTH.PUB.CL PUBLICATION.REF.SE SPEC.ED

General rules

Element

The allowed values, based on ISO 639-1, are listed in the t_language simple type. Various values are separated by ';'.
