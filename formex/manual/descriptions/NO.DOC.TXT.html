NO.DOC.TXT

[element]

Identifier of a document published in the table of contents of the OJ

The NO.DOC.TXT element is used to mark up the identifier (number) of a document published in the table of contents of the OJ.

Model

<xd:element name="NO.DOC.TXT" type="t_btx.seq" />

Used by

ITEM.VOLUME NO.DOC.TXT

General rules

Element

The element may only contain the document number in its textual format.
