IND

[element]

Indices

The IND element is used to mark up indices relating to a variable or an expression in a formula. There are two separate positions where indices could be related:

  • the index on the left, at the bottom,
  • the index on the right, at the bottom,

Model

<xd:element name="IND" type="t_btx.formula" />

Used by

t_btx.formula IND

General rules

Element

An indexed expression is understood to mean either an indexed variable or a complete indexed expression (marked up using the EXPR) element.

The IND element may contain one or more of the elements defined in the t_btx.formula complex type.
