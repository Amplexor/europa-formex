GR.NOTES

[element]

Group of notes

The GR.NOTES element is used to group together the notes referenced by callouts located in a table or in tabular structures in particular documents, such as the ones related to the varieties of agricultural plant or vegetable species, the ones related to the combined nomenclature etc.

Model

<xd:element name="GR.NOTES" >
  <xd:complexType >
    <xd:sequence >
      <xd:element ref="TITLE" minOccurs="0" />
      <xd:choice maxOccurs="unbounded" >
        <xd:element ref="GR.ANNOTATION" />
        <xd:element ref="LIST" />
        <xd:element ref="NOTE" />
        <xd:element ref="NP" />
        <xd:element ref="P" />
        <xd:element ref="TBL" />
      </xd:choice>
    </xd:sequence>
  </xd:complexType>
</xd:element>

Used by

t_btx t_btx.ecr t_btx.struct CAT.PLANT CAT.VEGET CN GR.PLANT GR.SEQ GR.TBL QUOT.S SPECIE TARIC TBL

General rules

Element

The GR.NOTES element may contain the following subelements:

  • an optional title (TITLE),
  • notes (NOTE),
  • lists (LIST),
  • numbered paragraphs (NP),
  • paragraphs (P),
  • groups of annotations (GR.ANNOTATION),
  • table structures (TBL).
