CONTENTS.SUMMARY.SE

[element]

Description of the body of the table of contents (special edition)

The element describes the body of the table of contents on the special edition cover page(s).

Model

<xd:element name="CONTENTS.SUMMARY.SE" >
  <xd:complexType >
    <xd:sequence >
      <xd:element ref="EDITION" />
      <xd:element ref="TI.COLL" />
      <xd:element ref="ITEM.SUMMARY" maxOccurs="unbounded" />
    </xd:sequence>
  </xd:complexType>
</xd:element>

Used by

SUMMARY.PDF

General rules

Element

The element contains:

  • the description of the edition (EDITION);
  • the title of the series (TI.COLL);
  • the list of items (documents) (ITEM.SUMMARY).
