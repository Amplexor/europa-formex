PAGE.HEADER

[element]

Current title for pages of th 'e-Recueil'

The PAGE.HEADER element contains the title which is placed at the top of all pages within a European Court Report document, as far it is published within the 'e-Recueil'.

Model

<xd:element name="PAGE.HEADER" >
  <xd:complexType >
    <xd:sequence >
      <xd:element ref="P" minOccurs="2" maxOccurs="unbounded" />
    </xd:sequence>
  </xd:complexType>
</xd:element>

Used by

CURR.TITLE

General rules

Element

The element is composed of at least two P elements.
