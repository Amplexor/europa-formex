FRACTION

[element]

Fraction in a mathematical formula

The FRACTION element is used to mark up fractions in mathematical formulas and expressions.

Model

<xd:element name="FRACTION" >
  <xd:complexType >
    <xd:sequence >
      <xd:element ref="DIVIDEND" />
      <xd:element ref="DIVISOR" />
    </xd:sequence>
  </xd:complexType>
</xd:element>

Used by

t_btx.formula

General rules

Element

The FRACTION element consists of these two mandatory elements:

  • DIVIDEND (DIVIDEND) for the part over the fraction bar,
  • DIVISOR (DIVISOR) for the part under the fraction bar.

Example

<FRACTION>
  <DIVIDEND>PCE
    <IND>red</IND>
 </DIVIDEND>
  <OP.CMP TYPE="EQ">  </OP.CMP>
  <DIVISOR>0,4 * PCE
    <IND>gross</IND>    <OP.MATH TYPE="PLUS">    </OP.MATH> 0,6 * NGR * PCE    <IND>gross</IND>
 </DIVISOR>
 </FRACTION>
